Dong Zhuo: My son Fengxian, why do you keep visiting the Minister's residence these days?

Dong Zhuo: Oh? I hear the Minister has an adopted daughter of remarkable talent and beauty...
Lu Bu: (nervously) You jest, father. My heart only seeks to assist you.
Narrator: (At this moment Diao Chan is brought into the court)
Dong Zhuo: (eyes lighting up) This must be the famous Lady Diao Chan?
